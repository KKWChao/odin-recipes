Pistachio Ice Cream Recipe

Serving Information

  • Prep: 15 Minutes
  • Cook: 15 Minutes
  • Additional: 1 Hour
  • Total Time: 1 Hour 30 Minutes
  • Serving: 12
  • Yield: 1 9 x 13 Inch Baking Pan

Ingredients

Crust
  • 2 Sleeves Buttery Round Crackers, Crushed
  • 1/2 Cup Melted Butter
Toppings:
  • 1/2 Gallon Vanilla Ice Cream, Softened
  • 2 (3 Ounce) Packages Instant Pistachio Pudding Mix
  • 1 1/2 Cups Milk
  • 1 (12 Ounce) Container Frozen Whipped Topping (i.e. Cool Whip), Thawed
  • 1/4 Cup Chocolate-Covered Toffee Bits (i.e. Heath), or to Taste

Instructions

  1. Step 1:

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit (175 C)

  2. Step 2:

    Combine cracker crumbs and melted butter in a bowl. Mix until evenly moistened; press into the bottom and sides of a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.

  3. Step 3:

    Bake in preheaded oven until crust is slightly browned and smells toasted, about 15 minutes. Remove from the oven and cool.

  4. Step 4:

    Mix ice crea, pudding mix, and milk together into a bowl until combined; spread evenly onto prepared crust. Spread whipped topping over ice cream mixture; top with toffee bits. Place in freezer until hardened, at least 1 hour
