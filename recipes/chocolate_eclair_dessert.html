Chocolate Eclair Dessert Recipe

Serving Information

  • Prep: 15 Minutes
  • Cook: 15 Minutes
  • Additional: 1 Hour
  • Total Time: 1 Hour 30 Minutes
  • Serving: 12
  • Yield: 1 9 x 13 Inch Baking Pan

Ingredients

  • 2 Individual Packets Graham Crackers
  • 2 (3 Ounce) Packages Instant Vanilla Pudding Mix
  • 3 Cups Milk
  • 1 (8 Ounce) Container Frozen Whipped Topping, Thawed
  • 1 (16 Ounce) Package Prepared Chocolate Frosting

Instructions

  1. Step 1:

    Line the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch pan with graham crackers

  2. Step 2:

    In a large bowl, combine pudding mix and milk; stir well. Mix whipped topping into pudding mixture. Spread half of a mixture over graham cracker layer. Top with another layer of graham crackers and the remaining pudding.

  3. Step 3:

    Top all with a final layer of graham crackers and frost with chocolate frosting. Regfrigerate at least two hours before serving to allow the graham crackers to soften.
